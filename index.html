We were approached by Henlow Church of England Academy when it became clear that they needed to expand the number of pupil places available. And because this is a ‘middle school’ Academy with children between the ages of 9-13, it was also necessary to upgrade and expand the indoor sports facilities too. Our design for a 4-court sports hall and attached 6-classroom block received planning permission from Central Bedfordshire Council. This was a large educational project which we oversaw from feasibility through to completion, with the design, specification, procurement and contract administration all undertaken by PCMS Design.
